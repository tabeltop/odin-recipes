Sandwich

Description

This is my daily driver when it comes to lunches. I have IBS and adhere to a Low Fodmap diet. This diet sadly restricts many ingredients, such as onions and garlic.

Ingredients

  • Sourdough bread
  • Turkey meat
  • Swiss cheese
  • Mayonase

Steps

  1. Toast two peices of Sourdough bread
  2. Once bread is toasted add cheese to one pieces of bread
  3. Put the turket on top of the cheese on the bread
  4. With the other piece of bread using a knife, add mayo to the bread
  5. Put mayo side on top of the turkey and cheese
  6. Enjoy!
